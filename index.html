About Done It

Done It is a powerful task management app with persistent reminders and smart recurrence. Never miss what matters with nagging notifications, flexible scheduling, and beautiful progress tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do nagging reminders work?

Nagging reminders send persistent notifications at your chosen interval until you complete the task. Perfect for important tasks you can't afford to forget!

What types of recurrence are supported?

Done It supports Daily, Weekly (specific days), Monthly (specific months), and Completion-Based recurrence. Premium users get access to all recurrence types.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. This will restore any previous premium subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

Can I sync across devices?

Yes! Sign in with Google or Apple to sync your tasks, tags, and settings across all your devices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Done It → Cancel Subscription.
